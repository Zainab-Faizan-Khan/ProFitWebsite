ProFit is a mobile application which gathers your personal body details and provides you with a customized workout plan. The application provides you with a perfect diet plan as well. ProFit keeps track of your fitness and daily routines. The application uses technologies like AI and Deep learning to guide you throughout the workout.

ProFit is the perfect assistant to help you reach your desired fitness goal. You can workout anywhere anytime with ProFit. The AI optimizes set, reps and weight for each excersise every time you workout. How cool is to have a personal AI fitness coach, fun right?

Special Features

ProFit's features are designed to help you improve your time body fitness and achieving a better physique. Get your personal customized workout and diet plans and meet your personal development goals with ProFit.

  • Customized Workouts

    ProFit provides customized workout plans. The users are provided with the correct way of exercising for some particular objective through a video and as users perform those exercises ProFit will guide them whether their posture is correct or not. Furthermore, ProFit also counts the number of times the user performs the specific part of the exercise.

  • Diet-Plan

    Users are provided a well tailored Diet-Plan according to the user's health status, weight and lifestyle, along with the user's weight loss and health goals. The diet plan acts as a bespoke template to steer your eating behaviour, exercise and lifestyle management towards optimal health and wellbeing.

  • Fitness Tracker

    A fitness tracker which uses sensors to track your orientation, movement, and rotation. It collects data and converts it into steps, calories, sleep quality and general activity you perform through the day.

  • Easy To Use

    ProFit provides you with an easy to use interface so that anyone can join the ProFit family!

  • Health Journal

    ProFit maintains a user profile, updates the Body mass index (BMI) and tracks the performances as you go on to the journey to a healthy lifestyle.
